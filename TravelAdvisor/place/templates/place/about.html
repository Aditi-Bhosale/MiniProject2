About Page

Eunuchs are often seen on the streets begging for money and are often discrimated against. we are living in the 21st century yet this part of our community is alienated.
EUnited is a small step towards the upliftment of this community.

This is a platform that aims to slightly normalize eunuchs living in our society. We offer job postings which can be put up by various organisations for the eunuchs, in fields not necessarily related to academics but any other field which requires skills which the eunuchs usually have or can develop easily. We offer a service where we can connect various users to buy products which the eunuchs make. The eunuchs can upload products or handicrafts which they wish to sell, and people who are interested can get in contact and buy them. We aim to bring a little financial stability and improved standard of living for eunuchs in todays world.
